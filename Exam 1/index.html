About Us

Here at Tito's, we like to create all your favorite Mexican cuisines from fresh ingredients. Rated as one of the top places to eat for 8 years in a row. We have a tight-knit crew who have all been working for us for a long time and everyday we serve up hundreds of happy customers.

Whether you want to throw a party here, in our private dining room, cater to you, sit down for a quick meal, or stay while at with a few friends or family, we have that ready for you. Plenty of TV's playing all your favorite teams! It's a great place to hang on gameday. Dont' forget to try some of our signature items. Also with tons of happy hour specials, dinner specials, and plenty of your favorite adult beverages, this is the place for you.
